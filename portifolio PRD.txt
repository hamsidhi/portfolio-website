AI Voice Agent

Designing Intelligent Conversations That Help Businesses Scale
Hero Description
Instead of replacing people, this project focuses on eliminating repetitive customer interactions through conversational AI.

The AI Voice Agent was designed for a real estate business to automate customer communication, qualify potential leads, answer frequently asked questions, and provide assistance 24 hours a day without requiring a dedicated calling team.

The objective was simple:

Reduce operational costs while improving customer response time and maintaining a professional customer experience.
Project Overview
Project Type

Artificial Intelligence

Industry

Real Estate

Status

Completed

Role

AI Conversation Designer
Prompt Engineer
Solution Designer

Platform

Vapi

Duration

Several days of prompt engineering, testing, refinement, and conversation optimization.
Business Problem
Most real estate agencies receive a high volume of repetitive customer enquiries every day.

Examples include:

• Property availability

• Buying process

• Renting process

• Selling property

• Pricing enquiries

• Appointment scheduling

• Lead qualification

Traditionally these conversations require dedicated staff members.

Hiring additional staff increases operational costs while limiting support to working hours.

The business wanted an intelligent solution capable of handling these conversations automatically while maintaining a natural and professional customer experience.
The Goal
Design an AI-powered Voice Agent capable of:

Answering common customer questions.

Collecting customer information.

Understanding customer intent.

Qualifying potential leads.

Directing customers toward appropriate next steps.

Operating continuously without requiring breaks or additional staffing.

Reducing the workload on human employees so they could focus on higher-value activities.
My Contribution
My primary responsibility was designing the intelligence behind the conversation.

Rather than developing a traditional software application, I focused on creating the conversational logic that guided every interaction.

This included:

Designing the complete prompt architecture.

Creating detailed business rules.

Defining conversation flows.

Handling objection scenarios.

Managing irrelevant or inappropriate user requests.

Improving response consistency.

Optimizing the conversation for natural human interactions.

Testing multiple conversation paths.

Refining prompts until the AI consistently produced accurate and professional responses.
Prompt Engineering
The most important part of this project was prompt engineering.

Instead of writing traditional application code, I designed an extensive instruction framework that defined:

The AI's personality.

Its communication style.

Business objectives.

Conversation rules.

Response priorities.

Lead qualification process.

Fallback behaviour.

Handling unexpected questions.

Professional tone.

Customer guidance.

The prompt evolved through multiple iterations, with continuous testing and refinement to improve conversation quality and reduce incorrect responses.

Handling Unexpected Questions
One challenge during development was ensuring the AI remained focused on its business purpose.

Customers sometimes ask unrelated or inappropriate questions.

The prompt was carefully engineered to:

Politely decline irrelevant requests.

Redirect conversations back toward real estate.

Maintain a professional tone.

Avoid hallucinations.

Prevent unnecessary conversations.

This significantly improved conversation quality while protecting the business experience.

Attendance records.

Assignment submissions.

Assessment scores.

Practical marks.

Course progress.
